## What changed from v3

- New section **5** (content licence): added a perpetual, irrevocable
  retention licence limited to legal/audit/regulatory/dispute-resolution
  purposes only — alongside the existing finite operating licence.
- New section **10A** (What we are NOT): explicit non-status as
  lawyer/accountant/financial advisor/broker/employer/etc.
- New section **10B** (What you do at your own risk): explicit
  user-responsibility for verifying contractors, jobs posters, in-person
  meetings, and any money exchanged.
- New section **10C** (Prohibited content): explicit zero-tolerance
  enumeration including immediate termination and reporting illegal
  content to authorities without prior notice.
